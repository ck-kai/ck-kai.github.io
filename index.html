Short Bio

​I am currently a third year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Qi Dou. Previously, I received the B. Eng. degree and M. Eng. degree from Wuhan University, under the supervison from Prof. Jian Yao.

My research interest lies in 3D vision, 6D object pose estimation, and robot manipulation.
